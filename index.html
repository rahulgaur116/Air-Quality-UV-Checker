Your Health, Your Life.

We are your one-stop daily check for environmental factors that cause or contribute to minor or chronic health conditions. Respiratory infections, asthma, and heart disease are a few examples. A simple daily habit on the top of your day can be the marker for a full day of adjusting how much time you spend outside and how the day may impact your skin and respiratory health. For your benefit we have also added a UV level checker so you can control of your skin health. According to the Skin Cancer Foundation the two major causes of skin cancer are the sun's harmful ultraviolet (UV) rays and using a UV tanning bed. Being that we cannot control what is occuring with our environmental factors, Prevention is key to not only keep your skin safe from skin cancer, but to ensure it stays healthy throughout your lifetime.

Preventative Care

Protection Measures

Air Quality: Worst-case scenario it is best to stay inside. Wear proper protection such as masks when going outside. If it applies on more chronic conditions: use portable oxygen pumps.

UV and Sun Protection: Proper protection such as wearing SPF 50+ hats and clothing. 50+ Sunscreen every day especially on ears and neck area. Additional environmental factors to consider are blue light from devices and even if you are indoors you can still recieve UV damage from light through windows. Coordinate proper times out of the sun and in shade. Stay as hydrated as possible.

Visit Your Doctor

For additional maintainance and care, Talk to your doctor or certified dermatologist. They can monitor any suspicious spots or health concerns that may best support your needs.
